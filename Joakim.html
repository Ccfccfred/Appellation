Joakim

Whats the origin

masc. proper name; a Joachimite (1797) was a follower of Italian mystic Joachim of Floris (obit c. 1200) who preached the reign of the Holy Spirit on earth, with a new gospel, would begin in 1260.

Meaning of the Name

"established by God"

"Jehovah raises up"
