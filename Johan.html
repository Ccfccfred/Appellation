Johan

Whats the origin

Scandinavian and Dutch masculine name meaning "Yahweh is gracious" or "God has been gracious". Originating from the Hebrew Yochanan and the Latin Iohannes, it is a common form of John. It is widely used across Northern Europe and is sometimes associated with the meaning "champion" in Malay.

Meaning of the Name

"Yahweh is gracious"

"God has been gracious"
