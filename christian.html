Christian

Whats the origin

520s as a noun, "a believer in and follower of Christ;" 1550s as an adjective, "professing the Christian religion, received into the Christian church," 16c. forms replacing Middle English Cristen (adjective and noun), from Old English cristen, from a West Germanic borrowing of Church Latin christianus, from Ecclesiastical Greek christianos, from Christos

Meaning of the Name

"A believer in and follower of Christ;"

"professing the "having the manner and spiritual character proper to a follower of Christ"
