Fatema

Whats the origin

feminine given name of Arabic origin. The name derives from the Arabic word 'fatama,' which means 'to abstain' or 'to wean,' indicating one who abstains from worldly desires or sinful acts. It carries significant historical and religious importance in Islamic tradition as it was the name of Prophet Muhammad's youngest daughter, Fatimah al-Zahra, who is revered as a role model of virtue, piety, and devotion.

Meaning of the Name

"One who Abstains"

"One who Weans"
