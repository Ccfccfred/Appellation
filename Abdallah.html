Abdallah

Whats the origin

Abdallah, an Arabic name meaning Servant of God or Slave of Allah. It symbolizes humility and submission to God's will, and has historical significance in Islamic traditions. Abdallah has been associated with influential figures and has gained recognition in other cultures due to migration and globalization.

Meaning of the Name

"Servant of God"
