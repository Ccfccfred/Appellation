Miriam

Whats the origin

fem. proper name,The name Miriam, rooted in ancient Hebrew, primarily means "bitter," "rebellious," or "sea of bitterness," reflecting the hardship of the Israelites in Egypt. It also signifies "wished-for child," "beloved" (from Egyptian roots), or "strong waters," with strong ties to leadership and faith
